Beachside Daiquiri

Description

Refreshing tiki flavors mingle in this delicious reinterpretation of the classic daiquiri.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces fresh pineapple-infused gold Barbados rum
  • 1 fluid ounce lime juice
  • fluid ounce orgeat syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime slice
  • 1 sprig fresh mint

Steps

  1. Combine Barbados rum, lime juice, orgeat syrup, and ice in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted.
  3. Strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with lime and fresh mint.
