Cuba libre

Description

Does it get any easier than this? It's rum and a cola beverage with a splash of lime.

Ingridients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 4 fluid ounces cola
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a 12-ounce glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the rum and cola beverage into the glass.
  3. Stir in the lime juice, and serve
